ETERNAL WOODS

An ecological memorial forest, taking shape among the trees of Southbury, Connecticut.

We are clearing paths by hand through quiet woodland to make a different kind of memorial: a living one. A camp-friendly forest where loved ones are remembered among growing trees — where remembrance looks like a trail walked, a night under canvas, a place that keeps living. Small by design. Slow by intention.
